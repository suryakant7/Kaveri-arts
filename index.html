The Artist

About Kaveri Kumari

Artist Photo

An artist from the heart, Kaveri Kumari transforms circles into universes, patterns into poetry, and colors into conversations.

With a foundation in Hindi literature — holding both a BA and MA in Hindi, and halfway through her B.Ed — Kaveri's journey took an unexpected turn toward the visual arts. What began as a quiet fascination with patterns blossomed into a passionate pursuit of Mandala art.

Each piece she creates is a meditation, a moment of stillness captured in intricate geometry and vibrant hues. Her work speaks of balance, harmony, and the infinite possibilities that emerge from a single point.

"Every Mandala is a journey inward. Every circle, a universe. Every color, a feeling waiting to be discovered."

Understanding

The Philosophy of Mandala

Sacred Circle

The Mandala, meaning "circle" in Sanskrit, represents wholeness, unity, and the cosmos. Each design begins from a central point, expanding outward in perfect symmetry — a metaphor for life's journey from the self to the universe.

Meditation & Mindfulness

Creating a Mandala is a meditative practice. Every stroke, every pattern requires focus, patience, and presence. The process calms the mind, dissolves stress, and invites clarity — transforming art into a spiritual experience.

Balance & Harmony

Mandalas teach balance — between chaos and order, complexity and simplicity, tradition and innovation. Through my art, I explore the delicate harmony between opposing forces, reflecting the dualities we all navigate in life.

Beyond the Canvas

Life, Family & Passions

A Proud Mother

Being a mother is my greatest masterpiece. The love, patience, and creativity I pour into raising my family mirrors the care I put into every Mandala. Motherhood has taught me resilience, joy, and the art of finding beauty in everyday moments.

Homemaker with a Creative Soul

My home is my sanctuary, where traditions thrive and creativity flourishes. From organizing spaces to crafting warm atmospheres, homemaking is another form of art — a canvas where memories are painted with love and care.

A Passion for Cooking

Food is storytelling. In the kitchen, I create flavors that connect cultures and hearts. My signature dishes — spicy, flavorful Chilli Paneer and Chilli Gobhi — are family favorites, crafted with the same attention to detail as my Mandalas.

"Art is not separate from life. It flows through everything I do — from the kitchen to the canvas, from motherhood to mindfulness."

Journey of Learning

Bachelor of Arts in Hindi

A deep dive into Hindi literature, poetry, and linguistic richness. This foundation shaped my appreciation for storytelling and cultural expression.

Master of Arts in Hindi

Advanced studies in Hindi literature, exploring classical and contemporary works. This academic journey deepened my connection to language and art.

B.Ed (In Progress)

Halfway through a Bachelor of Education degree, learning the art of teaching. Though paused, this path reflects my belief in lifelong learning and sharing knowledge.

Artistic Awakening

The discovery of Mandala art transformed my creative journey. What started as curiosity became a passion, and now a mission to share this ancient art form with the world.

The Future

Going Global with Mandala Art

My dream is simple yet profound: to take Mandala art beyond borders, connecting hearts and minds across cultures through intricate patterns and vibrant expressions.

01

Online Exhibitions

Hosting virtual galleries where art lovers worldwide can explore, appreciate, and acquire original Mandala artworks — breaking geographical barriers and bringing art to every screen.

02

Collaborations with Global Artists

Partnering with artists from diverse cultures to create fusion Mandala art, blending traditional Indian designs with global artistic influences — a celebration of unity in diversity.

03

Workshops & Teaching

Sharing the meditative joy of Mandala creation through online workshops, teaching techniques, philosophy, and mindfulness practices — empowering others to discover their inner artist.

04

Art Merchandise & Prints

Making Mandala art accessible through high-quality prints, merchandise, and home decor — allowing people to bring spiritual beauty into their daily lives.

"Art knows no boundaries. My Mandalas are whispers of the soul, and I believe they deserve to be heard across the world — one circle, one color, one heart at a time."
